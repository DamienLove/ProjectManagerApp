# Android Client Guide

The Omni Remote Android app provides a native mobile interface to your workstation.

### Connection & Cloud Sync
- **Firebase Auth**: Sign in with your authorized email to enable cloud features.
- **Fetch Cloud**: Automatically pull your `host`, `port`, and `secure` settings from Firestore.
- **Biometrics**: The app supports native biometric unlock for secure access to your workstation controls.

### Usage
- **Home Screen**: Quick access to the Terminal and Project dashboard.
- **Project List**: Toggle project status (Local/Cloud) with a single tap.
- **Remote Desktop**: Direct link to Chrome Remote Desktop for full UI control when needed.
