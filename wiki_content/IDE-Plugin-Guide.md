# IDE Plugin Guide

The Omni Remote Studio Plugin integrates remote management directly into your Android Studio or IntelliJ environment.

### Setup
1. Open the **Omni Remote** tool window (usually on the right sidebar).
2. Enter your agent's connection details.
3. Click **Test Connection**.

### Key Features
- **Project List**: See which projects are currently "Local" on your workstation.
- **Open in Studio**: Remotely trigger your workstation to open a specific project in the IDE.
- **Integrated Terminal**: Run remote commands without switching windows.
